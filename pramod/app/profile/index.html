A little about me.

I am a graphic designer from India. Born and brought
 up in Bombay, I’ve served in the Indian Army for seven years
 before changing battlefields for the world of advertising.
 There I found my calling in design.

I often find myself in the space that overlaps brand,
 design and communications, striving to find human solutions
 to business problems. And honestly, these days I am learning
 a lot about it from my three-year old son.

I’m now the design director at Wasabi Rabbit, New York.
 And I am excited to see where design takes me next.
 I am Pramod Maharana.
